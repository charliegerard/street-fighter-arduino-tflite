Using the Arduino Nano 33 BLE Sense and TensorFlow Lite, play this prototype of Street Fighter by moving your arms in the air.

To be able to try this experiment, make sure you have the TF4Micro Motion Kit installed on your Arduino. If you haven't already, follow the Getting started guide.

Sending Tensorflow model to your arduino via Bluetooth

Hold your Arduino in your hand, with the USB port facing down.

When you're ready, press start and throw a punch, shoryuken or hadoken in the air!
